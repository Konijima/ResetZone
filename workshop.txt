title=ResetZone - Scheduled Resets
description=[h1]ResetZone - Scheduled Resets & Loot Respawn[/h1]
description=
description=[b]ResetZone[/b] is a comprehensive server management tool designed for [b]Project Zomboid Build 42[/b]. It automates server restarts and intelligently resets specific map zones to ensure a continuous supply of loot for your players, all while keeping their bases safe.
description=
description=[h1]Key Features[/h1]
description=[list]
description=[*] [b]Automated Server Restarts:[/b] Keeps your server healthy by automatically restarting it every 6 hours.
description=[*] [b]Zone Management UI:[/b] An intuitive in-game interface allows Admins to instantly mark the current chunk as a "Reset Zone" or "Safe Zone".
description=[*] [b]Map Integration:[/b] Quickly manage large areas by holding [b]Shift[/b] and clicking or dragging on the World Map to mark/unmark zones.
description=[*] [b]Visual Feedback:[/b] Real-time UI indicators show the status of the zone you are currently in.
description=[*] [b]Pre-configured Zones:[/b] Includes a robust default list of popular loot locations ready to go.
description=[*] [b]Persistent Configuration:[/b] All your custom zone settings are saved server-side, ensuring they persist through restarts.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h1]Installation Requirement[/h1]
description=This is a [b]Hybrid Mod[/b] requiring both this Workshop item and a server-side Java Agent.
description=[b]The mod will not function by simply subscribing.[/b] You must perform a backend installation on your server.
description=
description=Files and setup guide available on GitHub:
description=[url=https://github.com/Konijima/ResetZone]https://github.com/Konijima/ResetZone[/url]
description=
description=[h1]How to Use[/h1]
description=[olist]
description=[*] Login to your server as an Admin.
description=[*] The [b]ResetZone UI[/b] will appear on your screen.
description=[*] Navigate to a zone you wish to manage.
description=[*] Click [b]"Mark Reset"[/b] to enable loot respawns for that chunk, or [b]"Unmark Zone"[/b] to protect it.
description=[*] [b]Pro Tip:[/b] Open the World Map (M), hold [b]Shift[/b], and click or drag to paint zones efficiently.
description=[/olist]
description=

description=[i]Essential for long-running servers to prevent loot exhaustion without the need for full map wipes.[/i]
